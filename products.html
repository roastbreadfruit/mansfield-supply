Products

Hardware

We are well stocked with fasteners of all sizes, 0-80 to 1", S.A.E. and metric, stainless and zinc-coated. If we don't have what you need, let us get it for you. Do you have a bike that you want to spruce up? We stock chrome nuts and bolts. Whether you need self-drilling, self-tapping or self-locking fasteners, we can help.

Tools

We have a great assortment of screwdrivers, including security drivers. Our hand tool selection at Mansfield Supply has everything from crowbars and vises to jack planes and power planes. Our tradesman quality tools come from names like Klein and Ridge. we have an extensive selection of small drivers, tools and accessories from name brands like Dremel and Rotozip

Seasonal

Spring

We carry a variety of annuals and perennials, herbs, and vegetables. We’ve got seeds and seed starters, potatoes and onion sets, fertilizers and spreaders. You can pick up your favorite mulch, and grab a new pair of pruners to give your landscaping a fresh look. On rainy days check out our extensive inventory of cleaning supplies (even sump pumps, if it comes to that)! It's also a great time to bring in your damaged screens and windows to be repaired by one of our experts.

Summer

Bring on the grilling! We have charcoal and gas grills, and everything you need to keep cooking your finest ribs. Before your next outing stop by for some coolers, tiki torches, lanterns, tarps, rope, and insect repellant. We'll sharpen your lawnmower blades, fix up the teeth on your chainsaw, and provide some snazzy tinted safety glasses to boot. Ever wanted to try canning? We've got jars, pectin, and an expert in the house!

Fall

When the weather starts to cool off and New England is putting on a show, take the time to enjoy it. But if it's your job to clean up the lawn, well, let us make that easier for you. Take a look at our selection of rakes, tarps, and the finest wheelbarrows around. If poison ivy is getting you down, pick up some relief and a new pair of work gloves. With the right perspective and a few good tools, autumn can be a beautiful thing.

Winter

Let it snow! You don't have to dread the winter. Keep yourself safe with ice melt, windshield scrapers, driveway markers, tube sand, and a good roof rake. Break that pesky shear pin on your snowblower? We have extra. And of course, we've got plenty of shovels, too. At the end of the day, grab a sled for the kids (or yourself—we won't judge) and a candy bar to help shake off those winter blues.
